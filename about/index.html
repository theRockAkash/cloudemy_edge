About Cloudemy Edge — Lucknow

We don’t teach courses.

Cloudemy Edge is a hands-on training lab in Lucknow, not a certificate factory. Students leave with apps in production, GitHub profiles that impress, and the skills companies in Lucknow and across India actually hire for.

Our Story

Built out of frustration with how coding is taught in India.

After years of working as an Android and Flutter developer, the founder of Cloudemy Edge saw the same problem repeatedly: students completing 6-month courses who couldn’t write a basic API call. Institutes were teaching theory, giving certificates, and sending students into interviews completely unprepared.

Cloudemy Edge was built to do the opposite. From day one, you write real code. Every week, you ship something. By graduation you have a Play Store app, a live website, or a deployed API — depending on your track. The certificate is the least important thing you leave with.
